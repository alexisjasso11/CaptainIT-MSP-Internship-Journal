## What I Learned
Shared mailbox configuration is one of the most common M365 admin tasks in any MSP environment — and getting repetitions on it is building real muscle memory. Permissions matter: Full Access, Send As, and Send on Behalf all behave differently and giving the wrong one can cause confusion for the end user. The more of these I do, the faster and more confident I get at navigating the M365 Admin Center without needing to look anything up.
