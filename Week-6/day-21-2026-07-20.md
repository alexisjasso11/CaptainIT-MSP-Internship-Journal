## What I Learned
Ansible is one of the most widely used automation tools in IT and DevOps environments — understanding how it works at a conceptual level is useful even before writing a single playbook. The core idea is the same as PowerShell scripting: stop doing the same task manually over and over, and let a script or playbook handle it consistently across every device. As environments scale up (more clients, more devices), automation becomes less optional and more essential. Good foundation to start building on.
