## What I Learned
Configuring a real client firewall and IPsec tunnel from scratch is not typical intern work — and doing it reinforced how much my home lab experience in pfSense has actually prepared me for production environments. The concepts (NAT, routing, firewall rules, encrypted tunnels) are the same even across different platforms. I also recognized today that studying wireless fundamentals proactively — before the interview, on my own time — is the same approach that's worked for me in the lab: build the foundation first, then apply it. The interview prep and the firewall config both showed that understanding *why* something works is more valuable than memorizing steps.
