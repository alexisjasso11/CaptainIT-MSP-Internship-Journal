## What I Learned
Not every issue gets solved in one sitting, and that's a normal part of hardware troubleshooting. A device showing a power light doesn't necessarily mean all of its components are functioning — power delivery to peripherals and display output can fail independently of the main power indicator. This is a good reminder to slow down and isolate each component (power supply, motherboard, ports) individually rather than assuming the whole system is "off" or "on" as one unit. Plan to revisit this with fresh eyes and possibly ask a coworker for a second opinion.
