# Captain IT Internship — Day 1 | Monday, June 16, 2026

## Day Summary
First day at Captain IT. Completed onboarding in the morning — reviewed company policies, signed paperwork, and read through multiple SOPs to understand standard procedures. In the afternoon, was assigned a real client task: setting up a laptop from scratch following a documented SOP, which included removing Dell bloatware and deploying Datto. The deployment alone took nearly 2–3 hours, which was a valuable early lesson in real-world IT timelines.
